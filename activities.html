Learn Mãori language

Māori, or te reo Māori is spoken by Māori people. It is recognised as one of New Zealand's official languages. Come learn Māori pronunciations and blend in with the locals.

Overnight stay at Tieke Marae

The Marae is a traditional meeting site for Maori’s and is an important part in Māori culture of community. It is a good opportunity to see local customs and immerse yourself into Māori culture and traditions. The Marae is also a hut where you can stay overnight in, offering an authentic cultural experience. Advanced booking is required to stay.

Kapa Haka peforming art

Kapa Haka is a traditional Māori performance. It means group dance. It is used to express their heritage and culture.

Māori arts

Learn Māori arts which includes weaving and carving.

The Māori Hangi

Come have a taste of hangi, which is a traditional Māori meal that is cooked by steaming food in a shallow pit. This method has been used before the European settlers.

Māori legends

Experience fascinating traditional Māori legends first hand.
